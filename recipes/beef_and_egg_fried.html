Beef and Egg Fried

This shit is delicous!

I often cook this myself and it is one of my favourite dish of all TIME!

Ingredients

  • Cow meat
  • Egg
  • Oil
  • White pepper

Steps

  1. Get 3 eggs and fried them
  2. Get the beef and season it with white pepper, some salt and some msg.
  3. Put it in the frying pan and cook it with vegetarian oil
  4. It is done!!!

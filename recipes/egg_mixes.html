Egg Mix

Just mix it according to your taste

It tastes so good with rice

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Crack 2 or 3 eggs and put it in a bowl
  2. Scramble the eggs
  3. Put salt, sugar and black pepper in the egg mix
  4. Put it in the frying pan and cook it
